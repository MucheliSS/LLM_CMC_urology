What LLMs Do Well

Capability Example for Urologists
Summarize & Synthesize Condense 20 papers on TURP outcomes into key themes
Generate First Drafts Create patient information leaflets, teaching cases
Provide Writing Feedback Review resident case presentations
Simulate Scenarios Role-play difficult conversations (e.g., cancer diagnosis)
Translate Complexity Convert AUA guidelines into trainee-friendly language
Deep Research Multi-source synthesis with citations (Perplexity, Gemini)

Where LLMs Fail

Limitation Why It Matters
Hallucinations Confidently states incorrect facts ‚Äî ALWAYS verify
No True Clinical Judgment Cannot integrate patient context like a clinician
Privacy Risks NEVER input patient-identifiable information
Superficial Engagement May shortcut productive struggle essential for learning
Algorithmic Bias Training data may underrepresent certain populations
Outdated Knowledge Training cutoffs mean recent guidelines may be missing
"The medical adequacy of LLM-generated answers can be significantly poorer than those from urology consultants ‚Äî human oversight is non-negotiable."

AI for Clinical Teaching

Teaching Task AI Application Key Tip
Case-Based Learning Generate clinical vignettes Be specific: specialty, level, objective
Formative Assessment Create MCQs, short-answer Qs Always review for accuracy
Feedback on Trainee Work Analyze presentations for gaps Use as co-reviewer, not sole evaluator
Reflective Practice Dialogic partner for reflection Complements, not replaces, mentorship
Patient Communication Simulate difficult conversations Safe space for practice

Practical Tips

  1. Prompt engineering matters: Role ‚Üí Task ‚Üí Format ‚Üí Constraints
  2. Model "AI skepticism": Show trainees how to verify outputs
  3. Create "productive friction": Don't let AI bypass necessary struggle

AI for Research

Research Phase AI Application Watch Out For
Literature Review Discover papers, synthesize themes Hallucinated references ‚Äî verify every citation
Protocol Development Draft methodology sections Requires human refinement
Data Analysis Generate R/Python code Verify code and outputs
Writing First Drafts Abstracts, intro sections Heavy revision needed ‚Äî never submit raw
Anticipate Reviews "What would reviewers criticize?" Strengthen weak sections proactively

Ethical Guardrails

  • ‚úÖ You remain responsible ‚Äî AI cannot be an author
